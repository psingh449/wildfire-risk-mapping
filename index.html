Wildfire Risk Mapping

Six block-group maps in a 3×2 grid. Drag to pan, scroll or pinch to zoom (all six stay in sync). Double-click any map to reset the view. EL (EAL, normalized for the choropleth) and RISC (overall risk) on the first row; hazard, exposure, vulnerability, and resilience on the next two rows. Hover any map for the same full-detail tooltip.

Validation summary

These values are computed during the pipeline and embedded in the county GeoJSON (rows 20–27 in calculations.csv).
External comparisons (FEMA NRI, MTBS) only become “real” when the corresponding external datasets are present at build time.
